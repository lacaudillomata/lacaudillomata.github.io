Initiatives

MDA Space Community of Practice on Machine Learning System Design
The CoP on MLSD is a community of practice that I spearheaded at MDA. It aims to assemble a cross-functional group comprising engineers, product developers, and technology experts. Its goal is to foster knowledge-sharing and best practices in Machine Learning System Design for next-generation Geointelligence products and services.
Last updated on May 25, 2024
RIIAA Community Circles (CIRCOS) Program
CIRCOS is an initiative I co-developed that evolved into a program held within the RIIAA conference. It serves as a platform for individuals across diverse sectors and disciplines within the Latin American AI ecosystem to exchange ideas and foster collaborations.
Last updated on Sep 30, 2022

UBC Science Leadership & Management (SLAM) Workshop Series
SLAM is an initiative I developed for the UBC Faculty of Science. It focuses on developing essential skills for success in scientific workplaces across academia and industry. Tailored for UBC grad students and postdocs in STEM fields, the workshops offer interactive, participatory experiences applicable to various career paths.
Last updated on Dec 31, 2018

CIMAT-Mitacs Globalink Partnership
I proposed this partnership to offer top CIMAT undergraduate students with summer research internships opportunities in Canada. With support from CIMAT’s director, we established the partnership, marking the first time Mexican institutions participated in the Mitacs Globalink program. This initiative began as a pilot and continues today, welcoming Mexican students across diverse fields, fostering valuable international collaborations.
Last updated on Jun 30, 2012
Website
CIMAT Industrial Problem-Solving (SPI) Workshops Program
The SPI program, which I co-founded at CIMAT, tackles real-world challenges encountered by both profit and non-profit organizations. In collaboration with academics and professionals, the program develops mathematical/computational use cases and feasibility solution studies through 1-week workshops. Over 4 years, I played a pivotal role in facilitating the program’s growth from local to international prominence, working closely with CIMAT’s exec team to enhance visibility and foster partnerships with Mexican industries. Today, SPI workshops are held annually as a flagship program at CIMAT’s Technological Service Office.
Last updated on Jun 30, 2011
